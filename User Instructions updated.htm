Features/Layers

·        Some of the features in the map are automatically turned on.  You can choose which ones you’d like to display on the map.

·        To turn the layers on or off, click the TOC tab and select or deselect your preferred layers.  Some layers will only be visible at certain zoom levels so if you can’t see what you’re looking for, try zooming in or out.

·        To go back to viewing the legend, click on the legend tab.

Zooming in

·        Use your mouse scroll wheel (if you have that kind of mouse); or

·        click on the + or – on the zoom bar (in the top left of the map) to go in or out.

·        If you get lost just type the name of the service or community in the search box and it will bring you back.

Search Box

·        To use the search function, simply type the name of a service provider or community into the box and a drop down list will appear. 

·        Some communities have multiple names and it will only search the official name so you may need to try alternative names. 

·        Service providers are listed under their full names so abbreviated names won’t work.  For example National Disability Insurance Agency will not be listed under NDIA.  You need to type the name in full.

 

More information

·        Click on any of the features or symbols in the map to get more information.  Depending on where you click you may get different information that you expected.  In the top right of the information box that pops up will be an arrow.  Click on that until you get to the information you wanted.  All of the features in the area you clicked will come up.  You can turn off layers (see Features/Layers) if you don’t want that information to appear.

·        Where the words are blue in an information box you can click for a link to further information in an external website.  It will open in a new window so you won’t lose the map.

·        You can also zoom to that feature on the map by clicking zoom to.

Service Providers

·        Service Providers are represented by blue stars.  To find them, either use the search box or click on where they are physically located.

·        A box providing information on the service will appear.  Where services have participated in this project, information about the service will appear.

·        There are some services who we have information about but who have not yet provided information to NDS NT on their services for inclusion in the map.

Spread of Service

·        Where we have information about the communities serviced by service providers we have demonstrated this with lines from the service to the community.  Hover over or click on the line from the service to see the services provided and to which community they’re going. 

·        Zooming out will give you a broader picture of the reach of that service.

Flood Zones

·        At this stage, flood zones are only available for the Katherine region.  These are major flood extents and indicate the likely spread and depth of water in the event of flooding.  For more information, click on the water.

·        To turn the flood layers on see Features/Layers.
